Important information for Registration

  • The deadline for registration can be seen in the important dates. It is important that at least one author per accepted paper registers on or before the deadline.
  • Authors registration includes the submission of up to six (06) pages for full paper.
  • At least one author must register and need to attend the conference to present the paper in person or virtual mode.
  • Participants/Attendees/Listeners are also invited.
  • Certificate of presentation shall be issued only if the paper is presented either in person or through virtual platform.
  • In no case, registration fee shall be refunded.

Guidelines Regarding On the Spot Registration

  • All the submissions done after 31st March, 2025(Last Submission Date on CMT Portal) shall be counted as On the Spot Registrations.
  • The submissions done on 18th & 19th April, 2025 will be counted only on the submission of Plagiarism Report (Turnitin Software, Similarity Index less than 10%), in Soft Copy as well as Hard Copy.
