About The Conference

The proposed International Conference on Smart and Sustainable Computing Technologies ICSSCT-2k25 will be held by Lingaya's Vidyapeeth , Faridabad, India to enable the researchers build connections between diverse sustainable technologies based on Multidisciplinary domains. Through sharing and networking, ICSSCT-2k25 will provide an opportunity for researchers, practitioners and educators to exchange research evidence, practical experiences and innovative ideas on issues related to the Conference theme. Smart and innovative computing solutions are key to addressing environmental sustainability, with emerging trends and applications in AI, Machine Learning, IoT, Blockchain, and AI-driven approaches focussiing sustainable agriculture.The event will feature keynote speeches, panel discussions, technical paper presentations, and interactive sessions, fostering collaboration and innovation. ICSSCT-2k25 focuses on promoting cutting-edge research and addressing challenges in smart computing, sustainability, and related domains.

Note: The manuscript submissions in this conference include the entire range of concepts from theory to practices, involving case studies, work-in progress and conceptual explorations..
