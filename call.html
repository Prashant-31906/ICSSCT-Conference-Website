Call For Papers

Lingaya's Vidyapeeth proudly hosts the "International Conference on Smart and Sustainable Computing Technologies (ICSSCT-2k25)" on 18th-19th April, 2025, in hybrid mode. The pivotal event aims to enhance sustainable practices within emerging markets through smart technology. It will bring together visionary industry leaders, influential policymakers, and renowned academicians to ignite discussions on pioneering multidisciplinary innovations for sustainable future. The conference will delve into how these technologiescan drive socio-economic growth in emerging economies, focusing on inclusion and environmental responsibility. Participants will gain valuable insights into the future of technology and sustainability, fostering a dialogue that bridges gap between advanced and developing regions.

The paper (pdf file) must be submitted electronically through Microsoft CMT (Submission link will be available once submissions are open).
